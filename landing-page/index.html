Frequently Asked Questions

How does Yoombaa work?

+

Yoombaa connects you directly with verified agents. Post your requirements, and agents with matching properties will reach out to you. Simple and efficient!

Why should I use Yoombaa?

+

All agents on Yoombaa are verified and vetted. This means you only deal with trusted professionals who have real properties to show you.

How are agents verified?

+

All agents must submit their ID and undergo verification before they can contact tenants. We also use a rating system so you can see what other tenants say about each agent.

Which cities do you cover?

+

We're now live in Nairobi and expanding to Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, Eldoret, and other major Kenyan cities throughout 2026.

What types of properties are available?

+

All types! From bedsitters and single rooms to 1BR, 2BR, 3BR apartments, townhouses, and maisonettes. Whatever you're looking for, we've got agents who can help.

How quickly will agents respond?

+

Most tenants receive responses within a few hours. Agents are notified immediately when a new lead matches their area, so you'll hear back quickly.
